About Me

Creative Content Writer!

With a wealth of experience, a mastery of language, and a gift for storytelling, I am a seasoned professional who can skillfully navigate the world of words. With over three decades of experience in Serbian/English interpretation, translation, content writing, and digital marketing, my journey has taken me from the vibrant streets of Johannesburg to the war-scarred landscapes of Bosnia-Herzegovina and Kosovo. As a skilled translator and interpreter, I have bridged linguistic and cultural gaps, facilitating effective communication in diverse settings. My expertise extends to empowering individuals with compelling resumes and CVs that showcase their unique skills and accomplishments. My relentless pursuit of academic excellence was enriched by the knowledge gained from a Master of Arts (MA) degree in cultural studies. From my captivating role as a Social Media Manager for the virtual home of South African wines - www.wine.co.za, to my expertise in crafting persuasive, concise, and impactful copy, I have honed my skills to guide brands towards unprecedented success. I bring a multifaceted skill set encompassing content writing, communication, social media, sales, marketing, and administrative management. As a content writer, translator, interpreter, and resume/CV writer, I am driven by the belief that words have the power to leave an indelible mark on hearts and minds.
